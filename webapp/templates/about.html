Registering as an Alumni Disclaimer

Alumni are encouraged to signup with a "work" telephone/mobile number and email. By work we mean a mobile number or email that can be posted publicly and can be contacted by any alumnus that ones has connected with. Since the numbers and emails will be displayed to fellow connected alumni, heinous actors (who may pose or be authentic fellow alumni) may use it and contact Alumni for nefarious reasons such as scamming and thus Alumni are encouraged to be cautious and diligent.

Information required:

  • Fullname (eg. Calvin Monteiro, Ayana Tesfaye, Julius Mandla Motsa, etc)
  • School (eg. University of Eswatini, Marwadi University, etc)
  • Programme (eg. MBA, MBA Finance, BSc Computer Science, PhD Physics, etc)
  • Year (eg. 1995, 2018, etc)
  • Mobile Number/Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Occupation (Software Engineer, Professor, Mayor, etc)
  • Organisation (Place of employment)
  • Country (Nation where based at)
  • Password (Should be more than 7 characters eg. C4l_()M0ntt)

Information that can be added after signup for a stronger profile:

  • Profile picture
  • Bio (eg. Write about your self, etc)
  • More schools, programmes and years if alumni of more than one school
  • Links to a personal website, repository, scholarly profile, etc
